Tips for best results

High contrast subjects

Photos where the subject stands out clearly from the background get the cleanest edges and most accurate cuts.

Use high resolution

Larger images (1000px+) give the AI more detail to work with, resulting in smoother, more precise edges around hair and fine details.

Good lighting helps

Well-lit photos with minimal shadows or blur allow the AI to identify subject boundaries more reliably. Avoid harsh backlighting.

Keep the subject centred

Cropping your photo so the main subject fills most of the frame before uploading reduces background noise and improves accuracy.
